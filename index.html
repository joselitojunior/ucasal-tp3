Interstellar

About the Movie

Interstellar is a science fiction film directed by Christopher Nolan. The story follows a group of astronauts who travel through a wormhole in search of a new home for humanity as Earth faces environmental collapse. The movie explores profound themes such as love, time, gravity, and sacrifice.
